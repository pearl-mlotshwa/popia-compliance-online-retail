# POPIA Compliance & Cybersecurity Risk Assessment  
### Online Retail Platform (Portfolio Project)

## Overview
This repository contains a POPIA-aligned privacy and cybersecurity risk assessment focused on an online retail environment.  
The project demonstrates the application of data protection principles, risk-based analysis, and governance considerations relevant to entry-level cybersecurity and GRC roles.

---

## Objectives
- Identify categories of personal information processed in the online retail platform  
- Conduct a high-level data flow analysis  
- Identify potential privacy and cybersecurity risk scenarios  
- Map identified risks to relevant POPIA conditions  
- Propose practical technical and organisational security controls  

---

## Scope
**Included:**  
- Online customer accounts  
- E-commerce transactions  
- Third-party payment processing  
- Delivery coordination with logistics partners  

**Excluded:**  
- Physical retail store operations  
- Internal human resource systems
